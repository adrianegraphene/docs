## Why FynCom?

**For Recipients:** Get paid to filter spam. Earn rewards for engaging with legitimate senders.

**For Senders:** Prove your intent with small, refundable stakes. Get refunded when recipients engage.

**For the Network:** Create economic barriers that eliminate spam while enabling legitimate communication.
